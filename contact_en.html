About us
Travel agency " TAJIK PEAKS " was created on behalf and support of the President of the Republic of Tajikistan, respected Emomali Rakhmon. TAJIK PEAKS is a modern company whose goal is a high-quality and modern approach to customer service. The audience of the site is mainly visitors who are interested in the natural and cultural features of our mountainous, sunny and hospitable country. During the time that we exist in the tourism market, hundreds of tourists have used our services and most of them have become regular customers and friends.
